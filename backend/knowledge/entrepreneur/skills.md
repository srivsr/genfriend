# Entrepreneur Core Skills

## Ideation & Validation
- Problem identification in target markets
- Customer discovery interviews
- MVP definition and rapid prototyping
- Market sizing (TAM, SAM, SOM)
- Competitive landscape analysis

## Product Development
- User story creation
- Prioritization frameworks (RICE, ICE)
- Agile/lean methodology
- Product-market fit assessment
- Iteration based on user feedback

## Sales & Marketing
- Cold outreach and prospecting
- Pitch development and refinement
- Content marketing basics
- Social media presence building
- Conversion optimization

## Financial Management
- Runway calculation and management
- Unit economics understanding
- Pricing strategy development
- Cash flow forecasting
- Bootstrap vs fundraise decisions

## Team Building
- Hiring early employees
- Culture definition from day one
- Equity compensation structuring
- Remote team management
- Wearing multiple hats effectively

## Resilience & Mindset
- Handling rejection and failure
- Pivoting when necessary
- Maintaining motivation during plateaus
- Work-life integration (not balance)
- Building support systems

## Networking
- Building genuine relationships
- Asking for introductions
- Providing value before extracting
- Maintaining a personal CRM
- Public speaking and visibility
