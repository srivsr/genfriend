# Entrepreneur Daily Habits

## Morning Routine
- Wake early for focused deep work
- Review metrics and customer feedback
- Prioritize 1-3 critical tasks for the day
- Exercise for energy (even 20 minutes counts)
- Avoid email/Slack until priority work is done

## Daily Practices
- Time-block for deep work (building product, writing, coding)
- Customer conversations (aim for 2-3 per week minimum)
- Sales activity daily (outreach, demos, follow-ups)
- Document learnings and decisions
- End-of-day inbox zero (or close to it)

## Weekly Rhythms
- Monday: Weekly planning and goal setting
- Friday: Week review and metrics analysis
- Weekly team standup (even with co-founder only)
- Content creation time (blog, social, newsletter)
- Networking event or coffee meeting

## Learning Habits
- Daily reading (30 min on industry, business, product)
- Podcast consumption during commute/exercise
- Twitter/X for real-time industry pulse
- Monthly deep-dive on one topic
- Learning from failed experiments (retrospectives)

## Financial Habits
- Weekly cash flow check
- Monthly P&L review
- Quarterly runway projection update
- Track personal burn rate
- Separate business and personal finances

## Health & Sustainability
- Non-negotiable exercise 4x/week
- Sleep protection (7+ hours)
- Regular social time (prevents isolation)
- Hobbies outside of work
- Quarterly personal retreat for reflection
