# Musician Core Skills

## Technical Mastery
- Instrument proficiency (scales, arpeggios, techniques)
- Music theory (harmony, rhythm, form)
- Ear training (intervals, chord recognition)
- Sight-reading ability
- Multiple instrument competency (optional but valuable)

## Performance
- Stage presence and audience connection
- Improvisation skills
- Managing performance anxiety
- Setlist curation and flow
- Collaboration with other musicians

## Production & Recording
- DAW proficiency (Logic, Ableton, Pro Tools)
- Home studio setup and acoustics
- Mixing basics
- Sound design
- Session musicianship

## Songwriting & Composition
- Melody development
- Lyric writing
- Arrangement skills
- Genre versatility
- Co-writing techniques

## Business of Music
- Streaming platform optimization
- Social media marketing
- Sync licensing knowledge
- Contract negotiation basics
- Building a fanbase

## Technology Skills
- Live sound basics
- Music software and plugins
- Content creation (video, audio)
- Website and email list management
- AI tools for music production

## Interpersonal
- Networking in music industry
- Collaboration with producers
- Working with managers/agents
- Fan engagement
- Taking and giving feedback
