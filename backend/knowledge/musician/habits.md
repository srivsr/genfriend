# Musician Daily Habits

## Practice Routine
- Daily practice (minimum 1 hour, ideally 2-4)
- Warm-up exercises (15-20 min)
- Technical work (scales, exercises)
- Repertoire building
- Creative exploration time
- End with something enjoyable

## Morning Routine
- Ear training exercises (even 10 minutes)
- Listen to new music across genres
- Physical stretching (musician ergonomics)
- Vocal care (for singers)
- Review goals and priorities

## Weekly Practices
- Learn one new song completely
- Analyze music you admire
- Record yourself and review
- Collaborate with another musician
- Social media content creation
- Administrative tasks (booking, emails)

## Creative Habits
- Songwriting sessions (scheduled, not just inspired)
- Capture ideas immediately (voice memos)
- Explore music outside your genre
- Attend live performances
- Create without judgment, edit later

## Career Building
- Daily social media engagement
- Weekly content release cadence
- Monthly email to your list
- Regular gig applications/outreach
- Relationship maintenance with venues/promoters

## Health & Longevity
- Hearing protection (always at loud gigs)
- Hand/arm stretches (prevent RSI)
- Vocal rest days (for singers)
- Sleep prioritization (creativity requires rest)
- Exercise for stage stamina
- Mental health check-ins

## Learning Habits
- Watch masterclasses and tutorials
- Study favorite artists' techniques
- Music theory deepening
- Technology and production skills
- Business of music education
