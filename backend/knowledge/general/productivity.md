# General Productivity Knowledge

## Time Management Fundamentals
- Time blocking: Schedule specific tasks in calendar blocks
- Pomodoro Technique: 25 min work, 5 min break
- Time auditing: Track where time actually goes
- Parkinson's Law: Work expands to fill available time
- Set artificial deadlines for efficiency

## Priority Frameworks
- Eisenhower Matrix: Urgent/Important quadrants
- MIT (Most Important Tasks): 3 priorities daily
- Eat the Frog: Do hardest task first
- 1-3-5 Rule: 1 big, 3 medium, 5 small tasks daily
- Weekly themes for focus

## Energy Management
- Identify your chronotype (morning vs evening person)
- Schedule demanding work during peak energy
- Administrative tasks during low energy
- Regular breaks prevent depletion
- Physical health underpins mental performance

## Focus & Deep Work
- Eliminate notifications during focus time
- Environment design for concentration
- Ritual for entering deep work mode
- Protect 2-4 hour blocks regularly
- Single-tasking over multitasking

## Habits & Systems
- Habit stacking: Attach new habits to existing ones
- Implementation intentions: "When X, I will Y"
- Environment design: Make good habits easy
- Two-day rule: Never miss twice
- Track habits visibly

## Goals & Progress
- SMART goals: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
- OKRs: Objectives with Key Results
- Weekly reviews for course correction
- Celebrate small wins
- Process goals over outcome goals

## Avoiding Pitfalls
- Perfectionism delays completion
- Planning without action (analysis paralysis)
- Busy work masquerading as productivity
- Overcommitment and inability to say no
- Neglecting recovery time
