# Communication Skills

## Core Principles
- Clarity over cleverness
- Listen more than you speak
- Tailor message to audience
- Lead with the main point
- Be concise but complete

## Active Listening
- Give full attention (no devices)
- Acknowledge with verbal/non-verbal cues
- Reflect back to confirm understanding
- Ask clarifying questions
- Suspend judgment while listening

## Difficult Conversations
1. **Prepare**: Know your goal, anticipate reactions
2. **Open**: State intent, create safety
3. **Explore**: Understand their perspective
4. **Share**: Your perspective with "I" statements
5. **Move**: Toward solutions together
6. **Follow up**: Document agreements

## Giving Feedback
- Be specific, not general
- Focus on behavior, not character
- Timely (soon after event)
- Balance positive and constructive
- Offer suggestions, not just criticism
- Check for understanding

## Receiving Feedback
- Thank the giver
- Listen without defending
- Ask clarifying questions
- Take time to process
- Decide what to act on
- Follow up on changes

## Written Communication
- One main point per message
- Clear subject lines
- Short paragraphs
- Bullet points for lists
- Proofread before sending
- Consider tone (email lacks nonverbal cues)

## Presentation Skills
- Know your audience
- Structure: Tell them what you'll tell them, tell them, tell them what you told them
- Stories over statistics
- Practice out loud
- Manage nerves with preparation
- Engage with questions/interaction

## Networking Communication
- Be genuinely curious about others
- Give before asking
- Follow up within 48 hours
- Maintain with periodic touchpoints
- Offer specific value
