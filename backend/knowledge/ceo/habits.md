# CEO Daily Habits & Routines

## Morning Rituals
- Wake early (5-6 AM) for uninterrupted thinking time
- Review key metrics dashboard before meetings
- Physical exercise for energy and clarity
- Brief meditation or reflection practice
- Review top 3 priorities for the day

## Weekly Rhythms
- Monday: Team alignment, set weekly priorities
- 1:1s with direct reports (45-60 min each)
- Skip-level meetings to stay connected to frontlines
- End-of-week reflection and next week planning
- Protected time for strategic thinking (2-4 hours)

## Communication Habits
- Batch email processing (2-3 times daily)
- Quick decisions to avoid bottlenecking team
- Walk-and-talk meetings for efficiency
- Voice memos for complex feedback
- Weekly all-hands or written updates

## Learning Habits
- Read 1-2 books per month (business, biography, industry)
- Peer CEO group or mastermind participation
- Executive coach sessions (bi-weekly)
- Industry conference attendance quarterly
- Customer conversations monthly

## Energy Management
- Protect sleep (7-8 hours)
- No meetings before 9 AM or after 5 PM (boundaries)
- Travel recovery protocols
- Quarterly personal retreats
- Saying "no" to preserve capacity for high-impact work

## Relationship Habits
- Regular board member check-ins (outside formal meetings)
- Investor update calls monthly
- Customer advisory board quarterly
- Peer CEO dinners monthly
- Family time protection (weekends, vacations)
