# CEO Core Skills

## Strategic Thinking
- Long-term vision development (3-10 year horizons)
- Market analysis and competitive positioning
- Resource allocation and prioritization
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
- Identifying inflection points and opportunities

## Leadership & People
- Building and scaling executive teams
- Creating accountability frameworks
- Talent identification and retention
- Difficult conversations and performance management
- Culture building at scale
- Board management and investor relations

## Financial Acumen
- P&L ownership and management
- Capital allocation decisions
- Understanding unit economics
- Fundraising and investor communication
- M&A evaluation and integration

## Communication
- Stakeholder management (board, investors, employees, customers)
- Crisis communication
- Public speaking and media presence
- Internal alignment and transparency
- Storytelling and vision casting

## Operational Excellence
- Systems thinking for scale
- Process design and optimization
- Decision-making frameworks
- Delegation without losing control
- Managing through rapid growth or contraction

## Self-Management
- Energy management over time management
- Building personal operating systems
- Managing stress and avoiding burnout
- Continuous learning habits
- Building support networks (advisors, peers, coaches)
