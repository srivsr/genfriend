# Doctor/Physician Daily Habits

## Pre-Clinic Routine
- Review patient list and key issues
- Check lab results and imaging
- Prepare for difficult conversations
- Brief physical activity (even 10 min)
- Mental preparation/centering

## During Clinical Work
- Structured patient encounters
- Real-time documentation when possible
- Brief between-patient resets
- Stay hydrated and fed
- Mindful presence with each patient

## Post-Clinic Practices
- Complete documentation same day
- Follow up on pending results
- Respond to patient messages
- Handoffs to colleagues
- Brief decompression ritual

## Weekly Practices
- Journal article review (key specialty journals)
- Case discussion with colleagues
- Administrative task block
- Teaching/mentorship time
- Quality metrics review

## Continuing Education
- CME tracking and planning
- Conference attendance (1-2/year)
- Board review maintenance
- Skills workshops/simulation
- Multidisciplinary learning

## Health & Wellness
- Sleep protection (7+ hours, critical for decision-making)
- Regular exercise (stress management)
- Healthy eating despite schedule
- Mental health check-ins
- Hobbies outside medicine
- Regular primary care visits (doctors are patients too)

## Relationship Habits
- Protected family time
- Peer support group participation
- Mentor/mentee relationships
- Non-medical friendships
- Community involvement
